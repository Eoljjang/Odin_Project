Nathan's Fried Rice!

Back to homepage

Description

Curated in the homes of many Asian households, fried rice is a given staple for many familes and individuals. It's a great way to get in lots of macro-nutrients while maintaining a great budget-friendly price and easy accessibility. Below is the steps to create my favourite version of fried rice.

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients and dice accordingly.
  2. Cook your meat in the pan first.
  3. Cook your vegetables in the pan next.
  4. Throw in your rice. Make a little opening and crack in an egg.
  5. Once the egg has firmed a little, break it up and mix it together with the rice.
  6. Add in a tablespoon of fish sauce, and 2 teaspoons of sesame oil.
  7. Mix well for 2 minutes - or until liking.
  8. Plate and serve.
